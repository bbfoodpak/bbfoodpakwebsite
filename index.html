about us

your one stop supplier

B&B Foodpak specialise in the supply of machine Knives and Replacement Parts for the Food, Packaging and Recycling Industries. In 2011 the acquisition of Andros Equipment extended our range of parts.

We have an extensive range of spare parts for the Food and Packaging industries. This includes Knives and some machine parts for Grinding, Slicing and Thermo Packaging Machines.

For further details you can view our Catalogue or download it here : BB-Foodpak-Catalogue.

We stock Slotting Knives, Razor Slitters, Pull Rolls, Creasers and No Crush Wheels for Corrugated Box manufacturers both in Australia and New Zealand.

Our high quality Granulator & Shredder blades are manufactured in EU and we have a large range available for most machines in the Plastic, Tyre, Metal and E-waste Recycling industries.
